📜 Game Guide

🎮 Controls

  • WASD / Arrow Keys — Move
  • Mouse — Aim & fire direction
  • Left Click — Dash (or fire if Click-to-Fire cheat on)
  • P / Escape — Pause
  • Insert — Spawn Player 2 (costs 1 life)
  • Mobile — Left stick: move · Right stick: aim · Double-tap right stick: dash
  • Gamepad — Left stick: move · Right stick: aim · RT: dash · Start/B: pause

⚔️ How to Play

  • Survive endless waves of emoji enemies.
  • Shoot enemies to earn XP — level up to choose a stat upgrade.
  • Collect 📦 boxes to unlock random power-ups.
  • Collect 🍎 apples (5 = +1 max heart & full heal).
  • Find the 🧙‍♂️ Merchant to spend coins on power-ups or XP.
  • Shoot 🛢️ barrels to trigger a fire explosion.
  • Enemies get faster and tougher as you level up.
  • A boss spawns every 11 levels — much bigger and tougher!

📈 Level-Up Upgrades

  • 🏃 Fast Runner — +8% movement speed
  • 🔫 Rapid Fire — +8% fire rate
  • 🧲 Magnet Field — +8% pickup radius
  • 💥 Increased Damage — +15% bullet damage
  • 💨 Swift Shots — +8% projectile speed
  • 💪 Power Shot — +8% knockback
  • 🍀 Lucky Charm — +0.5% drop rate

👾 Enemies

  • 🧟 Zombie — basic pursuer (lvl 1+)
  • 💀 Skull — faster pursuer (lvl 5+)
  • 🌀 Snail — slow, leaves slowing puddles (lvl 4+)
  • 🦟 Mosquito — erratic, leaves damage puddles (lvl 7+)
  • 🦇 Bat — stop-and-dash movement (lvl 10+)
  • 😈 Devil — moves on one axis at a time (lvl 12+)
  • 👻 Ghost — phases in and out, can't be hit while invisible (lvl 12+)
  • 👹 Demon — alternates chasing and random movement (lvl 15+)
  • 👁️ Eye — keeps distance and fires projectiles (lvl 20+)
  • 🧟‍♀️ Female Zombie — large and slow (lvl 25+)
  • 🧛‍♀️ Vampire — dodges bullets, drops diamonds (lvl 30+)
  • Boss — giant version of a random enemy, spawns every 11 levels
